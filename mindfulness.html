Happiness is a journey, not a destination

Mindfulness is a way of being more aware of the present moment. It helps us pay attention to our thoughts, feelings, and body sensations without getting caught up in them. When we practice mindfulness, we can feel more calm and relaxed.


This is easy to do and can be done anywhere. You can try closing your eyes and focusing on your breathing for a few minutes. Or, you can pay attention to the things you see, hear, smell, and touch around you.


It can help us feel less stressed and more happy. It's a good habit to practice every day. By being mindful, we can enjoy the present moment and feel better in our minds and bodies.
