Nothing is worth more than this moment

Hi! Let me tell you about Mindful Zensations. It's a website about two things that can help you feel better - mindfulness and meditation. Mindfulness means paying attention to your thoughts and feelings right now. Meditation means taking time to calm your mind and think clearly. Both can help with stress, focus, and feeling happy and calm.
