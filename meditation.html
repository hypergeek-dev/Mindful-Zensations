slow down and breath!

Meditation is a way to calm your mind and feel relaxed.
You can do meditation anywhere and at any time. All you need is a quiet place where you can sit or lie down and focus on your breathing.


When you meditate, you try to clear your mind and not think about anything else. This helps you feel more peaceful and calm.


You can start by meditating for just a few minutes each day and gradually increase the time as you become more comfortable with it.
